How it all started?

For the color palette I used the site "Colormind" its a site that generates colors for your site and from my third try I got the colors which I use for this site! It wasnt easy to find the right theme for the site but the blue colors of the site reminded me of Santorini. Then I added a few photos of Santorini to the site and the idea of creating a site about Santorini and the greek culture came to my mind. The design is created with the help of Bootstrap and obviously the site is created with HTML and CSS. The Bootstap is manually installed in the files of the site and everything is linked. This site also uses Sass for easier css management.
